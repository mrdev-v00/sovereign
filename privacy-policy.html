Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. Learn how Sovereign collects, uses, and protects your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sovereign ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot. By using Sovereign, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Sovereign collects the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

2.1 Discord Server Information

  • Server ID, name, and icon
  • Channel IDs and names
  • Role IDs and permissions
  • Server settings and configuration

2.2 User Information

  • User IDs and usernames
  • User roles and permissions within your server
  • Message IDs (for moderation and logging purposes)

2.3 Operational Data

  • Command usage statistics
  • Error logs and performance metrics
  • Temporary cache data for functionality

Important: We do not collect or store message content unless explicitly required for specific features (such as moderation logs or custom commands) that you enable in your server.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Purpose Information Used
Providing Bot Functionality Server ID, User IDs, Channel IDs
Moderation & Security User IDs, Message IDs, Server settings
Personalization Server configuration, User preferences
Analytics & Improvement Command usage, Performance metrics
Troubleshooting Error logs, Temporary cache data

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with limited access
  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary for bot functionality
  • Regular Audits: Our security practices are regularly reviewed and updated

Retention Period: We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you remove Sovereign from your server, associated data is scheduled for deletion within 30 days.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Exceptions include:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in operating our service (subject to confidentiality agreements)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have stored
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Stop using the bot or remove it from your server

To exercise these rights, contact us through our support server or via the contact information provided below.

7. Third-Party Services

Sovereign may interact with the following third-party services:

  • Discord API: For bot functionality and integration
  • Hosting Providers: For server infrastructure
  • Analytics Services: For performance monitoring (anonymous data only)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ. By using Sovereign, you consent to this transfer.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Last Updated: October 15, 2023
